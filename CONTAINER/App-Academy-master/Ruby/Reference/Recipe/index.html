Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups of Skim Plus milk
  • 100g frozen bananas
  • 3 tablespoons of PB2 peanut butter powder
  • 2 scoops of Chocolate Smooth MyProtein whey powder
  • 5 grams of creatine
  • Handful of ice cubes

Matt's Pro Protein Shake

Description

When you need the ultimate protein shake, you need this. It hits all the Macro requirements.

Directions

It doesn't get simpler than this. Blender required.

  1. Add 1.5 cups of milk (before the protein powder to avoid clumping)
  2. Add protein powder
  3. Add peanut butter powder
  4. Add creatine
  5. Add 3-4 ice cubes
  6. Blend
